Playstation Now Method

Prep time: 10-20mins weekly



Pick from over 300 PS4 games to download to your PS4 console and they’ll be ready to play, in up to 4K resolution if you’re playing on PS4 Pro.

Step 1: Grab your Phone/Laptop

Note: Requires minimal brain power

Open 3 incognito tabs on your browser and do not close them until the end
1. Email (for verification)
2. Playstation
3. Paypal (Playstation UK requires a payment method to subscribe for trial services)

Sign-Up Links:
Google
Microsoft
Playstation
Paypal

Save Credentials somewhere cause the human mind is a funny thing!

Step 2: Create a Google Account

Note: Create multiple PS Acc using same Email by making iterations distinguished by dots in between.

Lets take sofiasmith@gmail.com for example,
Sending an email to sofia.smith@gmail.com or even s.o.f.i.a.s.m.i.t.h@gmail.com will both reach the same address

Alternatively, use Microsoft accounts

Step 3: Create a UK Paypal Account

Postcode: SW1A 1AA

Important: Enter postcode manually, do not choose from dropdown list. Postcode is case sensitive.

Phone Type: Home (change from "mobile phone" to "Home")
Phone Number: +44 07XXXXXXXX

Step 4: Create UK Playstation Account

Province: England
Postcode: SW1A 1AA

Verify Playstation account

You are redirected to Playstation Website after verification

Step 5: Link Paypal to Playstation

a) Click/tap on your avatar (top right corner)
b) Payment Management
c) Add Payment Method
d) Add a paypal Account

Step 6: Add playstation account on Console

Sign in with Credentials registered during step 4

Open Playstation Store
Search "PS Now"

Choose Playstation Now: 7-Day Trial from search results

You can also add 2 weeks PS Plus on this account (To play Online on Main Account).
If you already have PS Plus, Skip to Step 7.

Go to Search on Playstation Store.
Playstation Plus: 14 Day Trial should be on Today's Top Searches & Subscribe.

Step 7: Reuse same Paypal account

At this point you can remove the payment method so it can be used again next time.

Head to Payment Management
Refer to Step 5

Remove the linked Paypal Account

Step 8: Set Account as Primary on your Console

> Settings
> Account Management
> Activate as Your Primary PS4
> Activate

Step 9: Download your games

Open PS Now on your home screen and download your games!

CRUTIAL:
Once downloaded, Open the game on the new account for around 30sec.
Log out of new Account (or not)

Log into your Main Account and start PLAYing

Step 10 lists how to continue playing the same games after trial ends without re-downloading the game and keeping your Game Saves

Step 10a: Renew Game Licences part1

Repeat Step 4 with 1 sole variation:
Add a dot in between your username part of email

e.g. sofiasmi.th@gmail.com
Complete Step 4

Repeat Step 5 to 8

Step 10b: Renew Game Licences Part2

Important: Open PS Now on new account. Look for your games (currently locked on Main Account)
Choose "Download Game" (Doesnt actually download game again, just renews licence)
Wait a few secs and your game is unlocked

Once Step 10b is done, you can delete the used disposable account (tidyness)
